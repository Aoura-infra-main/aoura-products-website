Red Wing Shoes — Since 1905 Founded in Red Wing, Minnesota, Red Wing has been making America's most trusted work boots for over 120 years. Their safety footwear meets ASTM standards and is relied upon on job sites worldwide — including across the UAE.

Why Choose Red Wing Safety Shoes?

Red Wing Shoes have been the global benchmark for industrial footwear for over a century. When working in demanding environments like oil and gas, construction, or heavy manufacturing in Dubai, you need uncompromising protection.

Safety Standards & Features

Our Red Wing collection meets strict international safety standards (ASTM, EN ISO). Features include:

  • Steel, Aluminum, and Non-Metallic (Composite) safety toes.
  • Electrical Hazard (EH) and Static Dissipative (SD) protection.
  • Puncture-resistant soles and slip-resistant outsoles.

Industries Served

We supply premium safety footwear to the construction, oil & gas, warehousing, logistics, and manufacturing sectors across the UAE.

Sizing & Care Guide

Red Wing boots are built to last, provided they are maintained. Clean your boots regularly with a stiff brush to remove dirt, and condition the premium leather to prevent cracking. Always select your true size, accounting for thick work socks.
