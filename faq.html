🚚Shipping & Delivery

Shipping & Delivery

We dispatch fast and deliver reliably across the entire UAE — with contracted carrier partners ensuring your orders arrive on schedule.

✅ Standard dispatch is within 1–2 business days of order confirmation.

Most orders placed before 2:00 PM (UAE time) on a business day are dispatched the same day or the following morning. Orders placed after 2:00 PM, or on weekends and public holidays, are dispatched the next business day.

In the unlikely event of an unforeseen delay — such as high-volume periods, logistics disruptions, or supply issues — we will notify you promptly by WhatsApp or email with a revised timeline.

We work with contracted, vetted logistics and courier partners across the UAE to ensure consistent, trackable, and timely delivery. Our carrier relationships are managed at the business level to maintain service standards.

Where applicable, you will receive tracking information once your order is dispatched. For B2B and bulk orders, our team coordinates delivery scheduling directly with you.

🎁 Free delivery on retail orders over AED 400 within Dubai.

For orders below AED 400, or for deliveries outside Dubai, a delivery fee applies. The exact fee depends on your emirate, order size, and weight. Contact us for a delivery quote on large or multi-location orders.

B2B and wholesale accounts may have separate delivery terms agreed upon at account setup. Speak to our sales team for details.

Yes — Aoura Products offers international export to select countries. International shipping is handled on a case-by-case basis and pricing depends on destination, order volume, and current freight rates.

Please contact us via WhatsApp or email to discuss international export requirements. We will provide a formal quotation including shipping costs, estimated lead times, and applicable documentation.

We take delivery commitments seriously. In the event of an unlikely delay — whether due to stock availability, logistics disruption, or force majeure — we will:

  • Notify you proactively via WhatsApp or email as soon as we become aware
  • Provide a revised, realistic dispatch and delivery estimate
  • Offer alternatives where possible (partial fulfilment, substitute products)
  • Escalate with our carrier partner to prioritise your order

We do not charge for delayed orders and will work to make it right.

↩️Returns & Refunds

Returns & Refunds

We stand behind every product we sell. Our return policy is designed to be simple, fair, and hassle-free.

✅ 7-Day No-Questions-Asked Return Policy on all eligible products.

If you're not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, you may return it within 7 calendar days of receiving your order — no questions asked.

Depending on the item and its condition, we will offer one of the following resolutions:

  • Full Refund — Returned to your original payment method
  • In-Store Credit — Applied to your Aoura Products account for future orders
  • Exchange — Swap for the same item or an equivalent alternative

Our team will advise on the most appropriate resolution based on the item type and reason for return.

Initiating a return is straightforward. Simply contact us within 7 days of receiving your order through any of the following channels:

  • WhatsApp: +971 52 150 3203 (fastest response)
  • Email: customer@aouragrp.com

Please include your order reference number and a brief description of the issue. You do not need to justify the return — simply let us know you'd like to proceed, and our team will arrange collection or advise on how to return the item.

Returns must be initiated within 7 calendar days of delivery. Items returned after this window may be assessed on a case-by-case basis.

For the smoothest return experience, items should ideally be returned in their original packaging and unused condition. However, we understand that sometimes you need to try a product before deciding.

Items that are eligible for return:

  • Unused or lightly used products in resalable condition
  • Defective or damaged items (received in poor condition)
  • Incorrectly shipped products (wrong item sent)
  • Items that do not match their description

Items that are not eligible for return:

  • Consumables that have been fully opened and used (e.g. ink cartridges, coffee capsules)
  • Custom or bespoke orders
  • Items damaged due to misuse or improper storage after delivery

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are typically processed within 3–5 business days. The time for the refund to appear in your account depends on your bank or payment method:

  • Bank transfer / credit card: 3–7 business days (bank processing times vary)
  • In-store credit: Applied immediately upon return approval

You will receive a confirmation message once your refund has been initiated.

We sincerely apologise if this has occurred. Please contact us immediately via WhatsApp with a photo of the item and your order reference. We will:

  • Arrange an immediate replacement or full refund — your choice
  • Organise collection of the incorrect/damaged item at no cost to you
  • Investigate and improve our fulfilment process
Quality issues are handled with priority. You will not bear any costs for our errors.
