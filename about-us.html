Our Story

Built to Solve One Problem

Running an office in Dubai means managing too many suppliers. One for stationery, another for printer consumables, a third for cleaning, someone else for coffee. Different invoices, different minimum orders, different delivery windows.

Aoura Products was built to fix that. We consolidated everything a modern office needs into a single, reliable supply relationship — 100+ products across 8 categories, under one account, one invoice, one delivery.

We operate as a division of Aoura Group, a Dubai-based trading company. With warehouses in Deira and Sajjah, Sharjah, we carry buffer stock across the most-used SKUs so your supply never stalls — even during peak seasons or short-notice orders.

Whether you're a one-desk startup or a 300-person corporate, the service is the same: fast, honest, and built around your business.
